    How We Bring Ideas to Life

    At JAG Drafting & Building Design, every project follows a clear, collaborative process ensuring accuracy, creativity, and efficiency from concept to completion.

    1

    Consultation & Concept We begin by understanding your vision, goals, and project requirements. Whether it’s a new build or renovation, we listen closely to translate your ideas into a solid design direction.

    2

    On-Page Optimization Our team creates detailed 2D CAD drawings and realistic 3D visualizations that bring your project to life ensuring every detail is clear, accurate, and ready for review.

    3

    Review & Refinement We collaborate with you to fine-tune the design. Every adjustment ensures your project aligns perfectly with your functional needs, aesthetic preferences, and compliance requirements.

    4

    Printing & Documentation With large-format digital printing, we produce high-quality plans and documents — ideal for on-site use, client presentations, or permit applications.

    5

    Permit Processing We assist in preparing and submitting all necessary documents for building permit approval, helping you avoid delays and streamline the construction process.
